  • Our Vission

We are christian outreach ministries worldwide, a church in Mombasa,Kenya in an area called Mweza.We carry out a church service on sundays from 4.00am-1.00pm.We also have a sundy school service.We take this chance to educate kids about God and encourage them to accept Jses as their personal saviour.We engage them in christian related activities such as saying memory versus and dancing for the Lord.We have our youths.They Lead the worship and praise session to keep them engaged with Christ in a personal level.

Our mission is to reach out to as many souls as possible to bring them back to christ. We do this by holding crusades, church conferences, youth conferences, bible studies and also sunday school services

It is our duty as saved christian to reach out others to help them know christ.By creating other branches of our church, we are reaching out to as many people as we can.

Our Vision is to reach un reached people in Kenya and Others countries of East and Central Africa with gospel of Jesus Christ, planting churches in the remotes villages, towns, as well carry on the teachings of the word of faith to these planted churches.

Gospel must be preached for it is the ONLY good News to this dying world, for Jesus commanded his followers to preach, and teach the word, we are 100% sure it works for since 1974 we had seen the results and we are ready to carry on for Jesus is LORD!!!.
